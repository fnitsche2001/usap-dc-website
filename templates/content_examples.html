Example 3:
For water sampling protocol, Limnological Methods for the McMurdo Long Term Ecological Research Program which is accessible online from url: https://www.montana.edu/priscu/documents/LTER-methods-web-page/Method_Manual_AC_22_Feb_2017.pdf.
Specific methodologies are described in the protocol book listed above and a general description is provided below.
Field chemical analyses: YSI 30 Sal/Cond/Temp; YSI 58 DO; Licor LI-1000 Data logger with 50 m cable
Licor LI-193SA spherical quantum sensor; Licor LI-190SA quantum sensor. General chemical analyses: dissolved inorganic nutrients (NH4, NO3- and PO4) were analyzed by Lachat autoanalyzer. DOC, DIC, and TN run on a Shimadzu TOC-VCPH (combustion catalysis), Ions on a ion chromatograph (Dionex). Absorbance measurements on Shimadzu 1601PC; fluorescence on Fluoromax 3 (Jobin-Yvon), Cary Eclipse, or Perkin Elmer LS50B; HPSEC Agilent 1100 Series HPLC; Primary amines and reducing capacity on Molecular Devices Spectra Max 340 (microplate abs reader).
