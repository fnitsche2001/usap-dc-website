How to Contribute Data

Help

Step #1: Deposit Your Dataset

USAP-DC accepts data submissions for any Antarctic datasets and provides repository services, including data publication in the DOI® system and long-term archiving.

Note: Contact us if other disciplinary repositories may be more appropriate for your datatype, e.g. those in this list.

Deposit

Step #2: Register Your Project

Register your project in the Antarctic Master Directory (AMD). Click on the "Register" button below to fill out a brief Web form, and we will create the AMD record required for your NSF final report.

Register

Step #3: Wait

Each AMD submission is verified by AMD staff and it takes usually several business days from submitting a record until a record goes live.
